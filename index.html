Assistant Professor of International Trade

School of Economics and Finance | Zhanjiang University of Science and Technology

I am an Assistant Professor of International Trade, specializing in international economics, gender-political economy, and regional industrial development.

My research focuses on global trade governance, gender inequalities in labor markets and global value chains, commodity market volatility, and sustainable agri-food systems.

Through this website, I present my academic work and share critical perspectives on contemporary economic and business issues, with particular attention to structural inequalities and policy innovation.

Research Interests

International Trade and Regional Economic Cooperation

In the field of international trade and regional economic cooperation, my research focuses on the impact of trade agreements like RCEP on regional trade competitiveness, agricultural commodity market dynamics, and swine industry economics, aiming to provide empirical basis for industrial upgrading and policy formulation.

Research
international-trade
Agricultural Economics and Industrial Organization

In the area of agricultural economics and industrial organization, I delve into structural issues and non-tariff barriers in export trade of products like tea and electromechanical goods, proposing targeted solutions.

Research
agricultural-economics
Global Commodity Markets and Trade Policy

Global commodity markets and trade policy are among my key interests, particularly the supply-demand changes, price volatility, and policy implications for agricultural products such as pork.

Research
commodity-markets
Women and Labor Market Studies

I also dedicate efforts to women and labor market studies, analyzing the impact of gender norms, institutional barriers, and social networks on women's career development.

Research
women-labor
Rural Revitalization and Sustainable Development

Regarding rural revitalization and sustainable development, I've participated in e-commerce curriculum reform and research on the vertical integration of the swine industry chain to promote smallholder sustainability and local economic transformation.
